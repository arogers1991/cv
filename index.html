Axl Floyd Rogers

I am a full-time PhD student studying Astronomy and Physics at AUT.
I also work part-time as an engineer at the Warkworth Radio Astronomical Observatory (WRAO).
My research involves the search for the stochastic gravtiational-wave background with Pulsar Timing Arrays (PTAs).
My work involves leading observations for geodessey and astrometry as part of the International VLBI Service (IVS).

Academic Background

  • Bachelors of Mathematical Science (B.Math.Sc) with a double major in Astronomy and Applied Mathematics, Auckland University of Technology, 2018.
  • Masters of Science Research (MSc) with First Class Honours, Auckland University of Technology, 2020.
